"Sing to the Lord a new song; sing to the Lord, all the earth. Sing to the Lord, praise his name; proclaim his salvation day after day."
-Psalm 96:1

We're building a family-friendly community where Chinese and Asian-Americans can build friendships, share life, and raise healthy kids.

As a Bible-believing Christian church, we'll explore what it means to "seek first the Kingdom of God" through the cultural lens of recovering Tiger Cubs finding our way in a performance-driven world.

Through our experiences, we've noticed that many Asian-Americans-even those who've lived all their lives in America-have a tough time finding a spiritual home in mainline Anglo churches. To fill this gap, we're shaping CECV to be a place where folks like us can "geek out" on God, grace, and good Asian food.

As a young ministry seeking to transform Clark County for Jesus Christ, we're on the lookout for co-workers, musicians and prayer warriors to join our team.

Current Ministries

Intercultural Marriages With the shrinking of the globe and the increase in connecting people via the internet, it is becoming common to find marriages that cross cultures. These marriages pose unique challenges, and we strive to encourage these couples with the heart to mentor and serve them.
Youth Motivated by God's love and empowered by the Holy Spirit, we seek to be a Biblically-based Youth Group that impacts our peers, our church, our community, and the world in ways that Jesus would. We have youth group on Friday nights and youth Sunday school after worship on Sundays. Middle school and high school students are welcome to join.
Children's Ministry Combined with the Chinese side. The children have their own Sunday school as well as Friday night programs. Once a month, we have a Children's Worship Service. We also have a nursery for babies and new mothers.
